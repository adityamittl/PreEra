Welcome to Pre Era
Our Vision

When the pandemic started, all the public spaces including Museums were shut. Since people could no longer visit museums, we brought the museums to people's homes.

Alternatively, we offer private tours of the museum for the extreme dinosaur lovers that you can attend from the safety of your home. The funds collected from it would serve as the only source of income during these tough times for the museum staff.
